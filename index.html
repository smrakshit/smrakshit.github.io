About Me

I am a Lead Wireless Research Engineer at Robert Bosch LLC with a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. My research focuses on developing innovative solutions in wireless communications, embedded systems, and IoT technologies.

Throughout my career, I have contributed to groundbreaking projects including Ultra-wideband (UWB) localization systems, autonomous navigation algorithms for lunar rovers, and low-power wireless networking solutions for industrial applications. My work has resulted in significant business impact exceeding €850M by 2030.

I hold multiple patents and have published extensively in top-tier conferences and journals. My research has been recognized with awards including CES Honoree status and performance recognition from Robert Bosch LLC.
